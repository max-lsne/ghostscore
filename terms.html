2. The Service

GhostScore provides a Digital Exposure Audit — a manual research service in which we scan 200+ publicly accessible sources to identify where an individual's personal information is publicly visible online. This includes:

  • Data broker and people-search websites
  • Search engine indexes (Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo)
  • Social media platforms (publicly accessible content only)
  • AI and large language model outputs
  • Data breach databases
  • UK public records (Companies House, Electoral Roll, court records)
  • Reverse image search results
  • Publicly accessible document metadata

The service produces a confidential PDF report containing a GhostScore (0–100 exposure rating), a source-by-source breakdown, and step-by-step GDPR removal instructions including pre-written letter templates.

GhostScore is an information and guidance service only. We do not undertake removal on your behalf. We do not provide legal advice. Removal instructions are provided as guidance based on our research experience.
